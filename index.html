    Native bash Jukebox based on cmd.fm

    How to

    1. chmod +x jukebox.sh
    2. ./jukebox.sh

    Usage argv

     -a         --> display all the musical genre .
 -g <style> --> launch a playlist the selected musical genre.

    Requirements

    1. mplayer
    2. curl
    3. Unix system
